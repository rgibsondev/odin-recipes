Toast


Description

Beautifully toasted bread.


Ingredients

  • Desired amount of slices of bread
  • Desired choice of topping (butter recommended)

Steps

  1. Using your desired choice of bread (we use wholemeal) place slices in the toaster or heat in a grill
  2. Toast until your desired degree of toastiness
  3. Add your favourite topping
  4. Enjoy!
