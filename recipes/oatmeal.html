Oatmeal


Description

Delicious oatmeal with blueberries to top.


Ingredients

  • 300ml water
  • 40g rolled oats
  • 60g blueberries

Steps

  1. Mix the oats and water in a pot and give a good stir
  2. Heat on a medium heat and bring to the boil
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Pour into your bowl and top with blueberries
