Apple Spinach Salad


Description

Healthy and tasty salad.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 medium apple, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons toasted pecans, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons KRAFT LIGHT DONE RIGHT! House Italian Reduced Fat Dressing

Steps

  1. Toast the pecans
  2. Wash the other ingredients
  3. Slice the apple and chop the celery
  4. Combine all ingredients when ready in a bowl with the dressing
